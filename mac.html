The BEST Buffalo Mac You'll Ever Make

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups uncooked macaroni
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbps all-purpose flour
  • 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp seasoning salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 cups shredded Mexican cheese
  • 2 cups buffalo wing sauce

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Bring about 4 quarts of water to a boil and add the pasta, let cook for 8-10 or until pasta is al dente.
  3. Bring an oiled pan to medium-high heat and add garlic and seasoned chicken. Cook thoroughly.
  4. In a seperate pot, set to medium-low heat, then prepare a roux by adding butter until melted. Gradually add flower and whisk until flour is cooked thoroughly or until roux is a caramel color.
  5. Add milk to the roux and stir carefully.
  6. Add softened cream cheese and stir until incorporated.
  7. Add shredded cheese and stir until fully incorporated.
  8. Add buffalo sauce, followed by cooked pasta and chicken.
  9. Let the base thicken on medium heat for about 5 minutes, season to taste.
  10. Add mixture to greased baking pan, layer the top with any extra cheese.
  11. Cover with aliminium foil and bake for 15 minutes.
  12. After 15 minutes, set oven broil to HIGH and broil top of pasta until a golden brown.
  13. Set your taste buds ablaze!
