My top 5 movies of all time

(in no particular order)

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Emotionally draining in the best way. One of Jim Carrey's best roles, in my humble opinion.

The Amazing Spiderman

I love Andrew Garfield's portrayal of Peter Parker.

Dreamgirls

Every song on the soundtrack is a certified bop. Also, Beyonce.

La La Land

Another great soundtrack. The ending isn't predictable but still satisfying.

Jeepers Creepers

This movie has great cinematogrpahy, great suspense and good character development.

That's all folks!
